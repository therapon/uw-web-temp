The goal of this class is to study the major areas under Software Engineering and how they are treated today in industry. Topics covered will include Design, Architecture, Development, Testing, Security, Privacy, Maintenance and others.

The class is structured around lectures by the staff, guest lecturers and panels from industry and class discussions/break-out sessions.
